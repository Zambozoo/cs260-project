Where

Spherus Magna

Once there were three celestial bodies: Bara Magna, Aqua Manga, and Bota Magna. When the MAKUTA was defeated by MATA NUI, all three combined to form SPHERUS MAGNA. Here, many races lived in harmony: Turaga, Matoran, Agori, Toa, Glatorian, and the many species of animal-like rahi. The Great Warrior too lived in a similar world, though his has since been decimated by his universe's MAKUTA.

Why

The Great Warrior

We are blessed that MATA NUI was able to defeat the MAKUTA, though the Great Warrior was not so fortunate. His TOA failed to assemble the golden armor and his universe was overun by darkness. He believe his only hope to save his friends is to achieve a comparable power to his adversary and steal as many legendary KANOHI as possible. He was strong enough to defeat the TOA MATA on his own, though not before the TOA could pass on their power to a new generation: the TOA MAGNA.
